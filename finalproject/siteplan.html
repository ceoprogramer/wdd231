Final Project - Site Plan

Site Name:

Live life travel Agency

livelife-travelagency.com

File: index.html

Site Purpose:

This project focuses on the development of a website dedicated to showcasing the tourist destinations offered by the travel agency "Live-life" organized by continent.

The main objective is to create a visually appealing and information-rich digital platform that motivates visitors to purchase one of the travel packages offered by the agency.

This website will provide info about diferents travel packages organized by continent:

  • Asia Travels
  • North America Travels
  • South America Travels
  • Europe Travels

Scenarios

1. A family or group of friends looking for info about travels packages

What touristic places can I visit by continent?

2. A couple or newlyweds who want to find a trip for their honeymoon.

Where can we find information about a trip to enjoy our honeymoon?
